## Overview

<a href="#">iMazing</a> delivers professional-grade iOS device management through an interface designed for both simplicity and depth. The application serves iPhone and iPad users, families, and IT professionals who need complete control over device content and backups.

The <a href="#">device browser</a> displays content organized by type for easy navigation. Find specific files, messages, photos, or app data through logical organization.

<a href="#">Automatic backups</a> protect device content continuously without manual intervention. Schedule background backups over WiFi that maintain current protection automatically.

<a href="#">Backup browsing</a> accesses content within backup archives. Extract specific files without full restore when you need individual items from previous backups.

The <a href="#">wireless connectivity</a> works with devices over WiFi without cable connection. Manage devices conveniently when they are on the same network.

<a href="#">Contact and calendar export</a> saves personal information independently of iCloud. Backup contacts and events locally for additional protection or migration purposes.

<a href="#">Health data access</a> exports health and fitness information from iOS Health app. Maintain personal copies of health records and workout history.

<a href="#">Voicemail extraction</a> saves voicemail messages as audio files. Archive important voice messages before they are deleted from carrier systems.

<a href="#">Ringtone management</a> installs custom ringtones without iTunes complexity. Create and transfer ringtones easily for device personalization.

<a href="#">Diagnostic information</a> reveals device details including battery health. Access information about device condition that iOS does not obviously expose.

<a href="#">Supervised device support</a> handles devices under MDM management appropriately. Work with enterprise-managed devices where applicable.
